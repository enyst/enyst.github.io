# Engel Nyst — Brand Guidelines

## The short version

**Aesthetic:** A builder's journal by lamplight.
**Voice:** Direct, technical, warm-but-spare, occasionally dry.
**Palette:** Dark workshop (#0f0f0e) with warm parchment text (#e5e0d8) and gold accents (#c9a84c).
**Fonts:** Instrument Serif for display, DM Sans for body, JetBrains Mono for code and labels.

## What the site is

An experimental public notebook. Architecture diagrams, investigation reports, working prototypes, data dashboards. Things under construction, labeled honestly with their status. The process is the content.

## What it isn't

A polished portfolio. A corporate presence. A blog optimized for engagement. The site has no analytics, no newsletter signup, no call to action other than the work itself.

## Visual identity

### Colors
- **Background:** Near-black (#0f0f0e) with subtle fractal noise texture at 3% opacity
- **Text:** Warm parchment (#e5e0d8) — not pure white, slightly yellowish
- **Secondary text:** Worn stone (#8a847a)
- **Cards:** Slightly lifted (#1a1a18) with subtle borders (#2a2a26)
- **Accent:** Warm gold (#c9a84c) — used for section headers and hover states
- **Links:** Cool mint/teal (#6bbfab)
- **Status tags:** Green for live (#7bc47f), teal for planned (#6bbfab), gold for data (#c9a84c), warm red for reports (#dc6b4a)

### Typography
- **Instrument Serif:** The name "Engel Nyst" at hero size. Large, elegant, unhurried.
- **DM Sans:** Everything you read. Body text, card descriptions, prose. 400/500/700 weights.
- **JetBrains Mono:** Section headers (uppercase, letterspaced), status tags, code, footer. The structural voice of the page.

### Layout
- 680px max-width for main content, 780px for architecture pages
- Generous top padding (100px) — the page breathes
- Cards with 6px border-radius, hover animation slides right with gold accent bar
- Single-column. No sidebar, no grid complexity.

### Motion
- Hero text fades up on load (600ms, staggered)
- Cards shift right on hover with gold accent reveal (200ms)
- No decorative animation. Nothing bounces.

The site is visibly, honestly under construction — because it is. Status tags, building-in-public energy, a cat as co-author. The imperfection is the point. See `magic_trick.md`.
